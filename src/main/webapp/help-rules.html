Specifies custom rulepacks or directories. When specifying a directory, all of the files in the directory with .bin and .xml extensions are included.

This field supports rulepack filesystem locations as well as URL's. The URLs may be to individual .bin or .xml rulepacks, or to .zip archives containing multiple rulepacks. The contents are downloaded, optionally unzipped, and placed into a temporary directory where they will be picked up automatically by Cloudscan.
